About Us

Mr. Ajeet Kumar Kesharwani has been grinding spices the same way for years — by hand, in small batches, with nothing added that wasn't already in the kitchen.

He and a small team of workers carry the spices themselves to countryside markets every week, standing behind every packet they sell. For anyone who can't make it to the market, the same spices reach the door through home delivery.
